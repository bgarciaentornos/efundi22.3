Purpose

The purpose of this content collection is to provide Sharable Content Object Reference Model (SCORM®) conformant examples and information using activities with the Plug-In Technologies, Flash and Director to developers using the specific technology. A plug-in technology is a language, tool, or environment not processed by the Web browser, but rather by a trusted third party provided as a “plug-in” to the browser’s environment. This content collection is targeted to developers already familiar with SCORM and at least one of the chosen plug-in technologies. The intention of this content collection is to familiarize developers with using a plug-in technology activity in context.
